# Code of Conduct

## TL;DR

Be excellent to each other. Disagreements about code or design are
fine and expected; personal attacks, harassment, or discrimination
are not.

## Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we
as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our
project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of
age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression,
level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion,
or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

Behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behaviour includes:

- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, personal/political attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate
  in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour
may be reported by emailing **`vbcherepanov+conduct@gmail.com`**.
All complaints will be reviewed within 7 days. Maintainers are
obligated to respect the privacy of the reporter.

[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.
